We are individuals who share common interests and put our ideas into existence in order to contribute to the Open Source. We try to change the perspective from ‘I don’t know’ to ‘I don’t know yet’. We, as MSTC, help aspiring developers with the right environment to execute their ideas. We aim to do this in as exciting and simplified manner as possible. We help people find answers to their questions, share ideas, solve problems and get more out of their ideas and technology. We also come up with various events and competitions which help them to create a strong foundation for their dream development programs and competitions like GSoC, Outreachy, Imagine Cup, etc.
